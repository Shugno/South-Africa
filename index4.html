South Africa is the southernmost country on the African continent. Their neighboring countries consist of Zimbabwe, Lesotho, Namibia, Botswana, Mozambique, and Swaziland.

All these neighboring countries have a good relationship with South Africa. It is also a popular tourist attraction that attracts a lot of people to go there. The economy of South Africa relies heavily on tourism and tourist attraction. South Africa has three cities as capitals which are Pretoria, Cape Town and Bloemfontein.
